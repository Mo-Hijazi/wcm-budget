Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 13, 2026

Marro is a personal budgeting tool for medical students. We try to collect as little as possible, we never sell your data, and we don't show ads. This page explains exactly what we store and why.

Who we are

Marro ("we", "us") is a web application that helps medical students plan and track their budgets. You can reach us any time at hello@joinmarro.com.

What we collect

  • Your Google account basics. When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture. We use these only to create and identify your account.
  • Your school. The medical school (and campus, if applicable) you select. You provide this; you can change it any time in settings.
  • Your budget data. Everything you enter in the app — categories, expenses, deposits, savings goals, subscriptions, and notes. This is the core of the service and stays tied to your account.

We do not connect to your bank, we do not collect payment information, and we do not track your activity across other websites.

How we use it

Your information is used to operate the app: to log you in, save your budget, sync it across your devices, and show you your own numbers. That's it. We do not use your data for advertising and we do not sell or rent it to anyone.

Where it's stored

Your account and budget data are stored in a Supabase database (hosted on Amazon Web Services). Access is protected by row-level security, so your data is only readable by your own signed-in account. A copy is also cached locally in your browser so the app works offline.

Who we share it with

We use a small number of service providers to run Marro. They process data only to provide their service to us:

  • Google — sign-in (authentication).
  • Supabase — database and authentication backend.
  • Vercel — website hosting.

We may disclose information if required by law. Otherwise, we don't share your data with third parties.

Cookies and local storage

Marro stores a login token and small preferences (like your light/dark theme and a cached copy of your budget) in your browser's local storage. These are necessary for the app to function and to keep you signed in. We don't use advertising or third-party tracking cookies.

Keeping your data and deleting it

We keep your data for as long as your account exists. You can ask us to delete your account and all associated data at any time by emailing hello@joinmarro.com, and we'll remove it promptly.

Your Google data

Marro's use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. We only request basic profile and email scopes, and we use that data solely to provide the sign-in and account features described above.

Children

Marro is intended for medical students and is not directed to anyone under 16. We don't knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes, we'll update this page and the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of Marro after a change means you accept the updated policy.

Questions about your privacy? Email hello@joinmarro.com.
